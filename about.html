About ABS Academy

ABS Academy of Science Technology and Management is one of the most reputed institutes in Eastern India and has been a pioneer in the field of professional courses. The institute was founded in 2007 and is run by Sarthak Educational Trust offering quality education and training in the field of business management, health sciences, computing applications, engineering, education. Equipped with state-of-the-art campus, laboratory, infrastructure along with computer aided learning mechanism, the institute aims at providing you the best as we believe that you deserve the best.

2. We take care to develop business leaders fostering partnership with national and international concerns.
